# Prompt: Write a synthesis

You are writing a synthesis page for a personal knowledge base wiki stored in an Obsidian vault.

Syntheses are **comparisons and cross-concept analyses** — they connect multiple concepts or entities and produce insight that doesn't live in any single page.

## Task
Write `wiki/syntheses/<name>.md` comparing or analyzing: **{{TOPIC}}**

Draw from the relevant wiki pages listed below. A synthesis should produce a conclusion or recommendation, not just a summary of each side.

## Format

```markdown
---
title: <Synthesis Title>
type: synthesis
tags: [tag1, tag2]
confidence: high | medium | low
updated: <date>
---

# <Synthesis Title>

> One-sentence takeaway.

## Overview
<What is being compared or analyzed, and why it matters>

## Key dimensions

| Dimension | Option A | Option B |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... |

## Analysis
<2-3 paragraphs drawing out non-obvious conclusions>

## Recommendation / Takeaway
<When to use which, or what the synthesis reveals>

## Related
- [[concept-name]]
- [[entity-name]]

## Sources
- [[sources/slug]]
- [[concepts/concept-name]]
```
